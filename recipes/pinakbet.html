Description

Pinakbet or pakbet consist of different kinds of vegetables. This dish reminds me of my childhood. It's our go-to meal. We had a garden on the front yard with different kinds of vegetables. It's the perfect dish you can ever make.

Ingredients

  • kalabasa(squash)
  • talong(eggplant)
  • sitaw(beans)
  • okra
  • ampalaya(bitter melon)
  • kamatis(tomato)
  • garlic
  • onion
  • ginger root
  • bagoong(fermented fish)
  • cooking oil
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. heat up pan, add oil
  2. saute garlic, ginger, onion and tomato
  3. add water and boil
  4. season the soup with bagoong
  5. first add the kalabasa (since it takes longer to cook)
  6. then add talong, sitaw, okra and ampalaya all together
  7. do not over stir to prevent mashing the vegetables
  8. season again to preference then serve

Description

This mouth watering dish origanated from the Philippines. You can make variety like pork and beef. It's good along with rice.

Ingredients:

  • chicken
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • onion
  • soy sauce
  • vinegar
  • fish sauce
  • cooking oil
  • bay leaves
  • salt
  • pepper
  • potatoes
  • water/chicken broth

Steps:

  1. season the chicken with salt and pepper
  2. add soy sauce and mix well
  3. marinate for 3 hours; the longer the better
  4. heat up a pan and put oil
  5. saute the garlic, ginger and onion
  6. add the chicken and cook for 5 minutes
  7. fill with water and add bay leaves
  8. bring to boil and stir
  9. lower to medium heat and cook for 20 minutes
  10. add 1 tbp of vinegar and 1 tbp of fish sauch
  11. cook for 10 minutes
  12. add the chopped potatoes
  13. once potatoes are cook, it's ready to serve

Spagetti

Description

I've always loved spagetti, well pastas in general. Jolibee had definitely been an influence. Having spagetti on the table is such a good treat

Ingredients

  • spagetti pasta
  • tomato sauce
  • tomato paste
  • onion
  • garlic
  • ground beef
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Steps

Pasta

  1. boil a pot of water
  2. add salt
  3. add a handfull of spagetti pasta
  4. keep stirring till till the pasta soft
  5. once the pasta is cooked, drain the water
  6. set aside cooked pasta and let it cool

Sauce

  1. get a pan and pour 5 tbp olive oil
  2. saute the onion and garlic
  3. add ground beef and stir for 5 minutes
  4. add tomato sauce and tomato paste
  5. keep stirring and cook for 15 minutes
  6. season with soal and pepper
  • mix the sauce and pasta on a plate, and add cheese of your own choice on top
